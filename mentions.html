It departs from classical mechanics primarily at the quantum realm of atomic and subatomic length scales. Standard Model provides a mathematical description of much of the dual particle-like and wave-like behavior and interactions of energy and matter. Standard Model provides a substantially useful framework for many features of the modern periodic table of elements including the behavior of atoms during chemical bonding and has played a significant role in the development of many modern technologies.
